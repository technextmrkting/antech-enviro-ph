Eight verticals. One point of contact.

Our equipment, consumables, and services show up across a wide range of industries in the Philippines — often within the same customer site.

Semiconductor

IDMs, OSAT facilities, and fabless test houses across the Philippines. We supply the failure analysis instruments, bond pull testers, probe cards, and reliability test chambers that keep these operations qualified and running.

Contract manufacturers and OEM brand owners running SMT and through-hole lines. We supply PCB assembly services, rework capability, production consumables, and the board-level repair that keeps lines moving when components fail.

Reliability qualification and failure analysis for AEC-Q100/Q200 components and assemblies. We supply the thermal shock chambers, HAST, and metallography systems that automotive-grade qualification programs require, alongside the IQ/OQ documentation auditors expect.
